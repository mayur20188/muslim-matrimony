Senior Brand Marketing Manager

Our growth in 2020 has been phenomenal – despite the pandemic, people are still looking for love and we're helping thousands of people around the world get married every month. We are building on our growth strategy and are looking for an equally phenomenal marketing manager that cares deeply about creating a brand that people love, that will help us build the muzmatch brand in a scalable way globally!

  • Possition : Full Time
  • Location : Flexible Remote/London
  • Salary : £50-65k
